    Mode: Offline
    Event Date : 03/04/23
    Event Time : 12.30 PM to 4.30 PM
    Venue : AB1-34
    Registration Fee : Rs. 100/-per team
    Eligibility
    Students from Engineering, Medicine, Arts and Science, and Diploma are welcome to participate in the events.
    Themes
    Gender Equality and Disability
    Sustainable Resilient Communities: Climate, Environment & Net Zero Targets
    Description A meme creation competition is a social media contest where participants create memes on platforms such as Instagram. It is a team event(MAX 4 participants per team).
    Competition will be based on the above mentioned themes.

    Rules
    Meme should not intend to hurt the sentiments of any individual, community, sect or religion. Any type of humorous consisting of the above-mentioned is not encouraged.
    The meme should be informative, entertaining, and fun, with twists having LOL and WOW factors.
    The meme should be made within 2 hours, as the TOPIC WILL BE GIVEN ON SPOT.
    Participants must bring their own devices (mobile, laptop) with software (editing apps, etc.,)
    Judging Criteria
    Relevance to the theme of the competition.
    Creativity and originality.
    Quality of the video.
    The effectiveness of the description in conveying the message of the video.
